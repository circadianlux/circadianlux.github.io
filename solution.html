  • Control the Melanopic lux ratio or Circadian stimulus

    Common lighting fixture contains light that impacts the circadian rhythm. But these fixtures are not optimised to support the circadian rhythm throughout the day. CircadianLux fixtures can adjust the melanopic lux ratio so that there is excellent quality of light to support vision and ability to adjust the circadian lux content throughout the day.​

  • Have natural, comfortable light based on your geo-location

    The right type of circadian lighting depends on the circadian day in your location. Other circadian lighting technologies use a one size fits all approach. CircadianLux optimizes for your actual geolocation, date and time to bring nature inside and complement nature.

  • Combine wellbeing with sustainability with human-centered, energy-efficient fixtures

    Tunable white fixtures offer little flexibility to adjust the melanopic lux ratio. To overcome this they have to install a large amount of tunable white fixtures which is not energy efficient. With CircadianLux the melanopic lux ratio can be adjusted with the right impact on the circadian rythm at the right time as well as providing excellent quality of light with low energy consumption. With LightScript™ algorithms these CircadianLux fixtures achieve 100lm/w compared to >65lm/w of other circadian lighting products.

  • Architectural lighting for professional projects

    Some companies promote single light sources such as bulbs, but these are creating a glaring work environment and are less suited for large scale architectural application. CircadianLux contributes to achieving building standards. Building standards such as the WELL Building seek to advance human health and wellness. The certification includes features and optimisations about circadian lighting. Ohter institues such as LRC RPI recommend the application of circadian stimulus.
